Kings Theatre Creative

My recent works are now on display in the Kings Theatre Creative gallery in the Kawakawa, Bay of islands.

The exhibition runs from 27th November till 20th December - you can find more about the event on facebook

Three Master artists explore their traditional practice and culture in sculpture, glass and paint. Ludek Adamek (Czech Republic), Anna Klekottka (Germany), and Beatrice Bourhis (Bulgaria) deliver a unique and diverse show, inspiring the imagination and a push to take old traditions to new places.
Showing 27 Nov - 20 Dec.
Open 5 Days Wed - Sun 10am - 3pm.
Kings Theatre Creative, 80 Gillies St, Kawakawa.
